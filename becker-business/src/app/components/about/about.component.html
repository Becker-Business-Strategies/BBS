About Becker Business Strategies
HISTORY
Stephen Becker is an accomplished senior executive with extensive experience leading industrial companies in both the US and international rail markets. He also has numerous Board roles in the global markets. Steve particularly excels in his ability to formulate strategies and implementation plans to enable North American firms to secure profitable and prominent entry in foreign markets. He has successfully demonstrated that capability in both high-growth, as well as mature markets. It’s been said that “Steve is equally at home in the Board room, as on the shop floor.” In 2003, Steve was recruited to run and build the international business for Amsted Rail, where as Managing Director, he was accountable for all business activities outside of North America. In 2017, he was assigned a similar role as Executive Vice President Transit to develop the Passenger Rail market for Amsted.
LEADERSHIP & EXECUTIVE
Widely acknowledged for his organizational development capabilities, including identification and career tracking of high potential individuals, Steve takes a keen interest in mentoring executives, and grooming his own successors. Steve has had full accountability for the profitable growth of $700 million revenue businesses, returning EBITDA margins above 20%. He has also configured successful Joint Venture operations in Brazil, India, China, Russia/CIS, and South Africa. Upon completion of his engineering studies, Steve began his career as a Project Engineer with National Castings, where he quickly moved up through the Operations ranks. While simultaneously completing his MBA, he was rewarded with Corporate Engineering, Technical Director, and Plant Manager responsibilities. His subsequent appointments included both Vice President and General Manager, Chief Technical Officer and finally Division President at ABC- NACO. There, his major change processes reduced manufacturing costs by 30%. He is credited for bringing Lean Manufacturing and Six Sigma techniques to the company.
